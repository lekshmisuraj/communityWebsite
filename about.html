Our Mission

Navakerala Malayali Association Inc. aims to empower, unite, foster growth, and transform lives within the Indian community. We are dedicated to fostering growth and development among individuals and families while channeling our resources to offer financial assistance to those who genuinely deserve it through various charitable initiatives.

Community-Centric Support

Creating a robust, interconnected community through educational programs, cultural events, and social initiatives.

Cultural Preservation

Preserving Kerala's rich cultural heritage through festivals, language classes, and traditional arts.

Empowering Growth

Our organization is dedicated to enabling individuals and families to reach their full potential. We provide access to resources, mentorship, and guidance that enable personal and professional growth, ensuring a brighter future for all.

Financial Assistance for Deserving Causes

We understand the importance of giving back to society by partnering with various charities and philanthropic organizations. Work diligently to provide financial assistance to those in dire need, contributing to positive change and lasting impact.

Collaboration and Outreach

We actively collaborate with like-minded organizations, businesses, and individuals to create a collective force for good. Together, we strive to address the challenges our community and the broader society face.

Our History

Navakerala is a non-profit community organization formed by the original Kerala settlers who relocated to South Florida in the early 1990s. Its objectives were preserving culture and traditions, creating a gathering place, and supporting quality of life.

The founders wanted to ensure that Kerala's cultural heritage and traditions, their place of origin, were maintained even as they settled in South Florida. Navakerala aims to preserve and promote its practices and culture in its new surroundings while creating a sense of community and support for themselves and future generations.

Navakerala is a hub for social interactions, cultural activities, and a support network for its members. Additionally, Navakerala serves as a source of support and solidarity for individuals and families far away from their home country.
