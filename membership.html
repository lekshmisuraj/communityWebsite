Why Join Navakerala?

By becoming a member of Navakerala, you join a vibrant community of Malayalis in South Florida who are passionate about preserving our culture and supporting one another.

  • Access to all cultural events and celebrations including Onam and Christmas
  • Networking opportunities with fellow Malayalis across South Florida
  • Youth and kids programs to keep children connected to their roots
  • Community support during times of need
  • Malayalam language classes for children
  • Sports and recreational activities including cricket tournaments
  • Tax-deductible membership fees (501(c)(3) organization)
  • Discounted rates for special events
  • Voting rights in association elections
  • Be part of charitable initiatives helping those in need

Membership Information

Annual membership is open to all Malayali families residing in South Florida. Please fill out the membership form and submit it along with the membership fee to join our community.
