Our Mission

Navakerala Youth is dedicated to engaging and empowering the young generation of our community. We provide a platform for youth to connect with their cultural roots while developing leadership skills.

Organizing volunteer activities and community service projects.

Leadership Development

Building leadership skills through organizing events.

Participating in cultural events and learning Kerala's heritage.
