A Legacy of Leadership

Since 1997, Navakerala has been blessed with dedicated leaders who have worked tirelessly to build and strengthen our community. Each president has contributed to making Navakerala the vibrant organization it is today. We are forever grateful for their service and dedication to the Malayali community in South Florida.

Navakerala

A non-profit 501(c)(3) community organization dedicated to preserving Kerala's rich cultural heritage in South Florida.
